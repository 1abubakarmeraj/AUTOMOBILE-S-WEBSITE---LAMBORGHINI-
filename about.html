Our Story

From a vision of excellence to a legacy of innovation

Founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini, Automobili Lamborghini was created out of a desire to challenge conventions and push beyond established limits. What began as one man's dream has evolved into a symbol of Italian excellence and automotive innovation.

Each Lamborghini is a masterpiece of engineering, design, and performance. From the iconic Miura that revolutionized supercar design to the cutting-edge Sián that incorporates hybrid technology, we have consistently redefined what's possible in the automotive world.

Our story is one of passion, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. We don't just build cars; we create dreams on four wheels that inspire generations and capture the imagination of enthusiasts worldwide.

Today, Lamborghini continues to push boundaries, combining breathtaking performance with sustainable technologies, ensuring that our legacy of innovation continues for generations to come.

The Lamborghini spirit is about more than just speed—it's about the relentless pursuit of perfection, the courage to challenge conventions, and the vision to create what others can only imagine.
